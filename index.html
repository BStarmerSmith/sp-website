Welcome to Suzy Parker Games

We make games that might ruin your evening, in the most entertaining way possible.

At Suzy Parker Games, we design brain-twisting, laugh-inducing, occasionally friendship ending games for people who enjoy a challenge... or just enjoy watching their mates squirm.

Our games can be played solo on a train, in a pub with a few friends, or during a full blown games night with 20 slightly confused people and one very smug winner. They're fun. They're frustrating. They'll probably keep you up at 3am wondering, “Why can't I stop thinking about that one stupid question?”

Blame Philip. He’s our co-founder and lead question-writer. After a lengthy stay in hospital (don’t ask), he decided to funnel his frustration into puzzles designed to gently torment innocent players. We tried to stop him. We failed.

Made by a bunch of creatives, techies and one dangerously enthusiastic project manager, Suzy Parker Games is here to bring you joy, chaos, and possibly a mild existential crisis.

If you’re a little bit weird, you’ll feel right at home.
